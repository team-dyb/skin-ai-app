What We Detect?

Powered by AI, our system detects seven major types of skin cancer with precision.

1. Melanocytic nevi

Melanocytic nevi, commonly known as moles

2. Melanoma

Melanoma is a serious form of skin cancer that develops in the melanocytes

3. Benign Keratosis-like lesions

Benign Keratosis-like lesions are non-cancerous growths on the skin that resemble melanoma.

4. Basal cell carcinoma

Basal cell carcinoma (BCC) is the most common type of skin cancer
