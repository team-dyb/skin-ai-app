About DetectDisease

DetectDisease is an innovative web application designed to assist in the early detection of skin diseases using advanced AI technology. Our mission is to empower individuals and healthcare professionals with accurate and timely information to promote better skin health.

By leveraging machine learning algorithms trained on thousands of dermatoscopic images, DetectDisease can identify various types of skin lesions, including melanoma and other common skin cancers. Early detection is crucial in improving treatment outcomes, and our platform aims to make this process accessible to everyone.

Our goal is to raise awareness, encourage early detection, and help users take informed steps toward better skin care.
