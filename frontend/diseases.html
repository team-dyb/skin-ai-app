What We Detect?

Powered by advanced AI, our system accurately detects seven major types of skin lesions, including melanoma and other common skin cancers.

Trained on thousands of dermatoscopic images, it analyzes subtle visual patterns invisible to the human eye - providing fast, reliable, and early insights to support dermatologists and clinical evaluation.
